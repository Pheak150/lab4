KampongSpeu Province

Welcom to KampongSpeu

Kampong Speu (Khmer: កំពង់ស្ពឺ, 'Port of Starfruit') is a province of Cambodia. It borders the provinces of Pursat and Kampong Chhnang to the north, Kandal to the east, Takéo to the southeast, Kampot to the south and Koh Kong to the west. Its capital is the town of Chbar Mon.
